# Hummus

## Info

- Time
  - 10 minutes
- Makes
  - 5 servings
- Macros/serving
  - 161 kcal
  - 6g protein
  - 14g carbs
  - 9g fat
- Tags
  - Snacks & small bites
  - Vegan
  - Vegetarian

## Ingredients

- 1 can (540ml) chickpeas (including half the liquid/aquafaba)
- 1 clove garlic
- ¼ cup tahini
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 tsp salt
- Naan (for serving)

## Instructions

1. Add all the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Serve with naan.

## Notes

- For a spicy/smoky hummus, add a couple chipotles in adobo sauce along with some of the adobo sauce.
