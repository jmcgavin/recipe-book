# Gochujang Turkey Rice Bowl

## Info

- Time
  - 30 minutes
- Makes
  - 3 servings
- Macros/serving
  - 538 kcal
  - 39g protein
  - 67g carbs
  - 9g fat
- Tags
  - Main course

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp neutral oil
- 450g lean ground turkey (or chicken)
- 2 green onions
- ¼ yellow onion, diced
- 5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 small knob of ginger, finely grated
- 1-2 mild chili peppers, thinly sliced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 3 cups cooked medium grain rice
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
- Soft-boiled eggs (for serving)
- Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

Sauce

- 2 tbsp gochugaru
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp mirin

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together the sauce ingredients and set aside.
2. In a pan heat 1 tsp of neutral oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground turkey and avoid stirring for a couple minutes to allow the bottom to brown a little. Flip and stir-fry until fully cooked and the liquid has evaporated.
3. Add the white parts of the green onions, onion, garlic and ginger. Sauté for 2 minutes.
4. Once the garlic turns golden brown, add in the sauce. Give it a good stir for 2 more minutes.
5. Add the remaining green onions, chili peppers, black pepper to taste, and sesame oil. Give it a final mix.
6. Divide the rice into serving bowls. Add a generous amount of turkey, and a soft-boiled egg. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Enjoy!
