# Mushroom and Leek Pasta

A Gordon Ramsay recipe.

## Info

- Time
  - 30 minutes
- Makes
  - 2-4 servings
- Tags
  - Main course
  - Pasta

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 8 oz mushrooms, trimmed and sliced (cremini or bella work well)
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 1 leek, quartered then chopped
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 8 lasagna sheets (or your pasta of choice)
- Â½ cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp roughly chopped tarragon leaves
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Steps

1. Heat a large frying pan and add a dash of oil. Season the mushrooms with salt and pepper and start to saute them, adding the garlic after 2 minutes and the leek a minute later. Cook for 6-8 minutes until the leek is soft and the mushrooms are colored on the outside Taste and adjust the seasoning.
2. Add the stock and boil for 5 minutes until reduced by half.
3. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna sheets in a large saucepan of boiling salted water until just al dente.
4. While the lasagna is cooking, add the cream to the frying pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes to reduce a little.
5. When the lasagna sheets are cooked, drain and add to the pan with the sauce, stirring until well coated. Turn off the heat, add the tarragon leaves, stir to combine, and enjoy!
