# Lemon Squares

## Info

- Time
  - 30 minutes
- Makes
  - 24 squares
- Tags
  - Dessert

## Ingredients

Crust

- 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 tsp lemon zest, grated
- 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature), diced

Filling

- 4 eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 tsp lemon zest, grated
- 1⅔ cup granulated sugar
- ⅔ cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
- 3 tbsp all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Lightly grease a 9x13 baking pan.
2. Prepare the crust by mixing together the flour, sugar, salt and lemon zest. Gradually add the butter mixing on low speed until the dough becomes crumbly.
3. Press the dough into the pan and freeze for 30 minutes.
4. Preheat oven to 350°F.
5. Prepare the filling by whisking together all ingredients.
6. Pour filling over the crust and bake until filling sets (about 25 minutes).
7. Cool completely. Cut and serve.
