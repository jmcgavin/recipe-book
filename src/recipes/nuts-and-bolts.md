# Nuts and Bolts

## Info

- Time
  - 1 hour
- Makes
  - ~7 cups
- Tags
  - Snacks & small bites
  - Vegetarian

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup salted butter
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp celery salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cups Shreddies
- 2 cups Cheerios
- 1 cup Cheezits
- 1 cup pretzels
- 1½ cup mixed nuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
2. In an extra large baking pan add ¼ cup of butter and place it in the preheating oven to melt the butter.
3. When the butter has melted, add the spices and Worcestershire sauce to the pan and mix.
4. Add in the cereal, pretzels and nuts and stir to coat in butter and spices.
5. Heat for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
