# Chili Cheese Pasta

With 42g of protein, 15g of fiber and less than 600 calories per serving, this is macro-friendly comfort food you can have every day.

## Info

- Time
  - 45 minutes
- Makes
  - 6 servings
- Macros/serving
  - 595 kcal
  - 42g protein
  - 70g carbs
  - 17g fat
- Tags
  - Main course
  - Pasta

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 poblano peppers, diced
- 1 large yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp diced chipotles in adobo
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 454g lean ground beef
- 1 can (540ml) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- 1 can (540ml) pinto beans, drained
- 1 can (540ml) lentils, drained
- Cilantro stems, chopped
- 2 cups beef bone broth
- 1½ cups water
- 340g pasta
- 1 cup sharp cheddar, shredded
- ½ cup 2% Greek yogurt
- Salt to taste

Chili seasoning

- 1 tbsp salt
- 1½ tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil in a large Dutch oven (or heavy-bottom pot) over medium heat. Add the diced poblanos and onions and sauté for a few minutes until the onions begin to brown.
2. Add the minced garlic, diced chipotle peppers and tomato paste and continue to cook for few more minutes.
3. Move the peppers and onions to the side of the pot to make room for the ground beef. Add the ground beef and allow it to sear on the bottom before mixing it.
4. Once the beef is fully cooked through, add the chili seasoning, can of tomatoes, can of pinto beans, can of lentils and some chopped cilantro stems. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
5. Add the beef bone broth, water and pasta to the pot. Cover with a lid and cook for 15-20 minutes.
6. Remove the lid and continue to simmer for another 5 minutes to allow chili to thicken.
7. Season to taste with salt. Add shredded cheddar cheese and Greek yogurt for creaminess. Mix until combined and enjoy!
