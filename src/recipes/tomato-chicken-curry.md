# Tomato Chicken Curry

## Info

- Time
  - 20 minutes
- Makes
  - 2-4 servings
- Tags
  - Main course

## Ingredients

- ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 2 cups tomato pasata
- 1 tsp ghee (neutral oil is fine)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Rice or naan (for serving)

Curry powder
- 2 tsp ground coriander
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground turmeric
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp garam masala
- ¼ tsp cayenne

## Instructions

1. Prepare the curry powder by mixing together all the spices.
2. In a large bowl, prepare a marinade by adding the Greek yogurt, lemon juice and half the curry powder. Add the chicken and toss to coat then place in the fridge for 30-60 minutes. Set the remaining curry powder aside.
3. Heat the ghee (or oil) in a pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 30 then add the reserved curry powder and bloom for 30 seconds before adding the chicken. Cook the chicken for a couple minutes on each side then add the tomato pasata and season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Simmer the chicken in the sauce for 10-15 minutes until fully cooked.
5. Serve with rice or naan.
