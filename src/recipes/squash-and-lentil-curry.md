# Squash and Lentil Curry

## Info

- Time
  - 6 hours
- Makes
  - 6-8 servings
- Tags
  - Freezable
  - Main course
  - Slow cooker
  - Vegan
  - Vegetarian

## Ingredients

- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 1 can (398ml) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (398ml) coconut milk
- 1 cup dried green lentils, rinsed and drained
- ½ cup dried red lentils, rinsed and drained
- 2 cups peeled, seeded and diced butternut squash
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1 tsp chili powder
- ¼ tsp ground cumin
- ¼ tsp ground coriander
- ¼ tsp ground turmeric
- Salt and pepper
- Naan (for serving)

## Steps

1. In the slow cooker, combine all the ingredients. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours. It can be maintained on warm for up to 8 hours.
