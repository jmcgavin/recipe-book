# Dad's Nuts and Bolts

## Info

- Time
  - 1 hour
- Makes
  - 7.5 cups
- Tags
  - Side dish

## Ingredients

  - ¼ cup salted butter
  - ½ tsp garlic salt
  - ½ tsp celery salt
  - ½ tsp onion salt
  - 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  - 2 cups Shreddies
  - 2 cups Cheerios
  - 2 cups pretzels
  - 1½ cup mixed nuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 230°F.
2. In an extra large baking pan add ¼ cup of butter and place it in the preheating oven to melt the butter.
3. When the butter has melted, add the spices and Worcestershire sauce to the pan and mix.
4. Add in the cereal, pretzels and nuts and stir to coat in butter and spices.
5. Heat for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
