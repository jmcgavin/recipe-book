# Sweet Soy Gochujang Beef
Korean-inspired braised beef.

## Info

- Time
  - 4 hours
- Tags
  - Slow cooker

## Ingredients

- 1kg beef chuck, cut into bite-sized chunks
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp gochujang
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- Rice, for serving

## Steps

1. Add all ingredients to slow cooker. Cook on high for 4 hours.
2. Serve with rice.
