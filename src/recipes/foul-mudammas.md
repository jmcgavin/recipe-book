# Foul Mudammas

Egyptian fava beans.

## Info

- Time
  - 30 minutes
- Tags
  - Side dish
  - Vegan
  - Vegetarian

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil (plus more for serving)
- 1 small red onion, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1 can (540ml) fava beans, rinsed and drained
- ⅓ cup water
- ¼ cup tahini
- Juice of ½ lime
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Naan (for serving)

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil in a pan over low heat.
2. Add the red onion, garlic and cumin seeds. Cook for 1-2 minutes to let the onions soften then add the tomato.
3. Once the tomatoes are soft, add the fava beans and water and simmer 5-10 minutes until it begins to thicken. 
4. Turn off the heat and add tahini, lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
5. Top with additional olive oil and serve with naan.
