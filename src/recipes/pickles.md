# Pickles

## Info
* Time
  * 20 minutes

## Ingredients
* 10 mini cucumbers, cut into spears
* 1 ½ cups water
* 1 ½ cups pickling vinegar (white vinegar is fine)
* 5 tsp kosher salt

Spicy Pickles
* 2 tsp peppercorn
* 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
* 2 tsp caraway seeds
* 1 tsp dry mustard
* Garlic cloves
* Jalapeno peppers, quartered

Dill Pickles
* 1 tsp sugar
* 2 tsp peppercorn
* 2 tsp caraway seeds
* 3 tsp dill seeds
* Fresh dill sprigs

## Steps
Spicy Pickles
1. Add water, vinegar, salt, peppercorns, crushed red pepper flakes, caraway seeds and dry mustard to a pot and bring to a boil.
2. When pickling liquid reaches a boil, remove from heat and set aside for a couple minutes.
3. Lighlty crush a clove of garlic and place into each jar along with a couple jalapeno slices.
4. Fill jars with cucumber spears and then cover with pickling liquid. Ensure cucumbers are fully submerged in pickling liquid.
5. Gently twist the lids onto the jars and allow them to cool to room temperature before placing in the fridge.

Dill Pickles
1. Add water, vinegar, salt, sugar, peppercorns, caraway seeds and dill seeds to a pot and bring to a boil.
2. When pickling liquid reaches a boil, remove from heat and set aside for a couple minutes.
3. Lighlty crush a clove of garlic and place into each jar along with a couple sprigs of dill.
4. Fill jars with cucumber spears and then cover with pickling liquid. Ensure cucumbers are fully submerged in pickling liquid.
5. Gently twist the lids onto the jars and allow them to cool to room temperature before placing in the fridge.

## Notes
* <u>These pickles are not shelf-stable</u>. But they will last for several months in the fridge.
