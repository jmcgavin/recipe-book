# Lasagna

## Info

- Time
  - 1 hour
- Tags
  - Main course
  - Pasta

## Ingredients

- [spaghetti-sauce]
- Fresh mozzarella cheese, grated
- Ricotta cheese
- Lasagna sheets, cooked

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. In a baking dish, layer ingredients in the following order: sauce, lasagna, mozzarella, ricotta. Repeat as many times as the height of your baking dish will allow.
3. Cook for 40 minutes.
4. Broil for 3-5 minutes until top cheese layer is golden-brown.
5. Let cool before serving.
