# Breakfast Sausage Seasoning

The perfect ratio of herbs, spices, and brown sugar combine to make the best breakfast sausage seasoning for patties and links! It works great with ground pork, turkey and chicken.

## Info

- Time
  - 5 minutes
- Makes
  - ~3 tbsp
- Macros/serving
  - 60 kcal
  - 1g protein
  - 12g carbs
  - 1g fat
- Tags
  - Other

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp fennel seeds
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp packed brown sugar
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- ½ tsp ground sage
- ¼ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients

## Notes

- Use approximately 1 tbsp to season 1 pound of ground meat.
