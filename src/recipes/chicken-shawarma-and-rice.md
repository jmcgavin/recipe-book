# Chicken Shawarma and Rice

## Info

- Time
  - 4 hours
- Makes
  - 4-6 servings
- Tags
  - Main course

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken thighs, tenderized
- 1 large yellow onion
- Tzatziki sauce (for serving)

Sauce

- 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ground coriander
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp black pepper

Rice

- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- ½ tsp ground turmeric
- ¼ tsp ground cumin
- 1½ cups long-grain or Basmati rice
- 2½ cups chicken broth
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

Sauce

1. Mix all sauce ingredients together.
2. Use half the sauce to marinade the chicken for 1.5 hours. Keep the other half of the sauce for serving later. Place the chicken and sauce in the fridge.
3. When chicken is done marinating, begin preheating oven to 400°F.
4. Cut yellow onion in half and place flat portion on baking dish. Poke a skewer through onion and stack chicken thighs on skewer like a vertical rotisserie. Top with the remaining onion half. Bake for 1 hour 45 minutes.

Rice

1. Melt the butter over medium heat in a large Dutch oven. Add the turmeric and cumin and cook until fragrant but not browned, about 1 minute.
2. Add the rice and stir to coat. Cook, stirring frequently, until the rice is lightly toasted, about 4 minutes.
3. Add the chicken broth. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Raise the heat to high and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce to a simmer, and cook for 5. minutes without disturbing. Remove from heat and allow to rest until the water is completely absorbed and the rice is tender, about 10 minutes.

Serving

1. Slice chicken and onion halves. Serve over rice with dollop of your reserved sauce and some tzatziki sauce.
