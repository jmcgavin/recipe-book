# Taco Seasoning

Basic taco/fajita seasoning.

## Info

- Time
  - 5 minutes
- Tags
  - Other

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ¼ tsp crushed red pepper flakes (or pinch of cayenne, to taste)
