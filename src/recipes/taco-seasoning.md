# Taco Seasoning
Basic taco/fajita seasoning.

## Info
* Time
  * 5 minutes

## Ingredients
* 1 tbsp chili powder
* 2 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp salt
* ½ tsp paprika
* ½ tsp dried oregano
* ¼ tsp garlic powder
* ¼ tsp onion powder
* ¼ tsp crushed red pepper flakes (or pinch of cayenne, to taste)
