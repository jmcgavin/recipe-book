# Spaghetti Sauce

## Info

- Time
  - 4 hours
- Makes
  - ~7 cups
- Tags
  - Other

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 454g medium ground beef
- 1 Italian sausage, casing removed
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 onions, diced
- 1 can (156ml) tomato paste
- 1 can (796ml) crushed tomatoes
- 1 can V8 vegetable juice (or 1 can tomato sauce)
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, add 2 tbsp of olive oil.
2. Add the ground beef and sausage and allow the bottom to sear before mixing in the garlic and onions.
3. Add the tomato paste and continue to cook with the meat and onions for a few minutes.
4. Add the crushed tomatoes and can of V8 along with the Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Mix to combine.
5. Put a lid on the pot and bring to a boil then turn the heat to low and let it simmer for 4-5 hours.
