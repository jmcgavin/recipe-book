# Korean Pork and Slaw Rice Bowl

Korean-inspired pork and veggie rice bowls.

## Info

- Time
  - 30 minutes
- Makes
  - 4 servings
- Tags
  - Main course

## Ingredients

- 450g ground pork (or chicken/turkey)
- 400g coleslaw mix (shredded green cabbage, red cabbage and carrots)
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 5-8 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- 1 cup basmati rice
- Black pepper, to taste

Sauce

- 2-3 tbsp gochugaru (Korean chili pepper flakes)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp mirin

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together the sauce ingredients and set aside.
2. To a pan add 1 tbsp of oil and heat over medium-high heat.
3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute until they get a little bit of color.
4. Add the ground pork and black pepper to taste. Break up the pork and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until no longer pink.
5. Reduce the heat to medium and add in the sauce. Continue to cook until pork is fully cooked, stirring occasionally.
6. Add the coleslaw mix to the pan (it will seem like a lot but it will shrink as it cooks down). Mix to combine and continue to cook on medium heat until the coleslaw has softened.
7. Season to taste with black pepper.
8. Serve in a bowls with an equally-sized portion of rice. Mix together and enjoy!
